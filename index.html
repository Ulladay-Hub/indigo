Indigo Terminal

An advanced GUI terminal application for developers and power users.

About

Welcome to Indigo Terminal! Developed by Sirbradinator and contributors, Indigo Terminal is designed to provide a modern and intuitive terminal experience with a graphical interface.

Indigo Terminal supports a variety of commands including printing messages, performing arithmetic operations, navigating directories, and more. It is built to enhance productivity and ease of use for developers and enthusiasts alike.

Contributors

This project is maintained by Sirbradinator and contributors from the open-source community.
